My research in clinical trials focuses on the development of adaptive designs and platform trials, utilizing sequential decision-making and multi-armed bandit models to improve efficiency and patient-centricity.

<section id="software" markdown="1">
## Software Packages
* **ConDistris (2025):** Designing clinical trials using confidence distributions (R and Excel).
* **OneArm (2023):** R Shiny App for designing one-arm trials with dichotomous endpoints.
* **NCC (2023):** CRAN R package for simulation and analysis of platform trials with non-concurrent controls.
* **SIMPLE (2023):** R package for modular and efficient simulation of complex platform trials.
* **BinaryBandit (2019):** Julia package and R Shiny App for evaluating finite-horizon bandit problems.
</section>
